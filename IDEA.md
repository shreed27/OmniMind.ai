# Vision

OmniMind.ai is **not** another AI chatbot.

It is **not** another multi-agent framework.

It is the world's first **Operating System for Autonomous Organizations.**

Instead of asking AI to answer questions, users give OmniMind a **Mission**.

The operating system autonomously creates an organization around that mission, delegates work, creates temporary specialists when necessary, executes tasks, reflects on failures, learns from experience, evolves its organizational structure, and continuously improves over time.

The organization—not the individual AI agent—is the unit of intelligence.
