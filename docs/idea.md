# OmniMind.ai — Idea

## One-line pitch

OmniMind.ai builds an autonomous organization around every objective.

## The problem

Companies are slow because humans must coordinate humans.
Chatbots are shallow because they answer one question then stop.

## The insight

The organization — not the individual agent — is the unit of intelligence.

## The vision

Users give OmniMind a **Mission**.
It creates a temporary company: CEO, departments, teams, managers, workers.
It executes, reflects, learns, and evolves structure automatically.

## The feeling

After the demo, users should say:
> "This doesn't feel like I'm using AI.
> It feels like I'm watching an autonomous company think."
