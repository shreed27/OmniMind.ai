# Vision

OmniMind.ai is the world's first Operating System for Autonomous Organizations.

It is NOT:

- another chatbot
- another AI assistant
- another multi-agent framework
- another workflow automation platform

Instead, OmniMind creates an autonomous organization around every objective.

Every objective becomes a Mission.

Every Mission creates a temporary living organization capable of planning, debating, executing, learning and evolving.

Instead of asking

> "How can an AI solve this?"

OmniMind asks

> "What organization should exist to solve this?"

The organization becomes the intelligent system.

# EnterpriseOS Kernel

The EnterpriseOS Kernel is the heart of OmniMind.

The kernel is NOT an AI model.
