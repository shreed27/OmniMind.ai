# Product Name

OmniMind.ai

Tagline

The Operating System for Autonomous Organizations

---

# Product Vision

OmniMind.ai transforms artificial intelligence from a chatbot into a living organization.

Users no longer interact with individual AI agents.

Instead, every objective becomes a Mission.

Every Mission automatically creates an autonomous organization capable of planning, delegating, debating, executing, learning and evolving.

The organization itself is the intelligent system.

---

# North Star

If a DeepMind engineer finishes the demo, they should say

"This doesn't feel like AI.
It feels like I'm watching an autonomous company think."

---

# Problem Statement

Today's AI systems

- Answer questions
- Generate code
- Execute workflows

But they don't behave like organizations.

Real organizations

- Plan
- Delegate
- Coordinate
- Debate
- Learn
- Improve

OmniMind brings organizational intelligence to AI.
